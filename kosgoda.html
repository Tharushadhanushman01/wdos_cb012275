From shell to shore: Sri Lanka's dedicated turtle hatchery

Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Welcome to the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, a haven dedicated to the conservation and protection of Sri Lanka's remarkable sea turtles. Nestled along the pristine shores of this tropical paradise, our hatchery stands as a beacon of hope for these magnificent creatures. Step into a world where nature's miracles unfold before your eyes. Witness the delicate process of nesting as mother turtles carefully bury their precious eggs beneath the warm sands. Experience the joy of watching hatchlings emerge, their tiny flippers propelling them towards the boundless ocean. At Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, we are more than just guardians; we are custodians of a timeless legacy.
Join us in our mission to preserve these ancient mariners, ensuring that future generations can marvel at their beauty. Together, let us forge a path towards a brighter, more sustainable future for our cherished sea turtles.
