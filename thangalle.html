From shell to shore: Sri Lanka's dedicated turtle hatchery

Thangalle Turtle Hatchery

Welcome to Thangalle Turtle Hatchery, a haven for Sri Lanka's magnificent sea turtles. Nestled along the pristine coastline of Sri Lanka, Thangalle Turtle Hatchery is dedicated to the preservation and protection of these remarkable creatures. Step into a world where conservation meets wonder as you embark on a journey to witness the marvels of nature unfold before your eyes. Experience the thrill of witnessing the gentle giants, such as the Green Turtles and Leatherback Turtles, as they lay their precious eggs on our sandy shores. Immerse yourself in the incredible life cycle of these ancient creatures, from the moment they hatch to their courageous journey back to the ocean. Our passionate team of conservationists works tirelessly to ensure the safety and well-being of these endangered species. Through education, research, and community engagement, Thangalle Turtle Hatchery is committed to creating a sustainable future for our beloved sea turtles. Join us in this extraordinary endeavor as we strive to protect and preserve these incredible beings for generations to come. Discover the magic of Thangalle Turtle Hatchery and become part of our mission to safeguard these living treasures of the sea.
